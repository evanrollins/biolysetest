Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 4, 2024

At Biolyse Pharma Corp., your privacy matters to us. This policy outlines what information we collect when you use our website, why we collect it, and what we do with it.

What Information We Collect

Information You Give Us

When you contact us, apply for a job, or request product information, you might provide:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Company name and professional title
  • Your message or inquiry details
  • Resume and work history (if applying for a position)

Information We Collect Automatically

Like most websites, we collect some technical information automatically:

  • Your IP address and general location
  • Browser type and device information
  • Which pages you visit and how long you stay
  • How you found our website

We use standard cookies to improve your experience and understand how people use our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

How We Use This Information

We use your information to:

  • Answer your questions and respond to inquiries
  • Send you information you've requested about our products
  • Review job applications and schedule interviews
  • Improve our website based on how it's being used
  • Keep our website secure and prevent fraud
  • Meet our legal and regulatory obligations

We won't use your information for anything you haven't asked us to do.

Who We Share Information With

We don't sell your information to anyone. Period.

We only share your information when:

  • You ask us to — For example, if you request product information that requires coordination with a distributor
  • We work with service providers — Companies that help us run our website or send emails on our behalf. They're contractually required to keep your information confidential
  • The law requires it — If we receive a valid legal request or need to protect someone's safety
  • Our business changes — If Biolyse is acquired or merged, your information would transfer to the new owner

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. This includes secure servers, encrypted connections, and limited employee access.

That said, no internet transmission is completely secure. While we do our best to protect your information, we can't guarantee absolute security.

How Long We Keep Your Information

We keep your information only as long as we need it. For example:

  • Contact form inquiries — Typically 2-3 years or until the matter is resolved
  • Job applications — Up to 2 years in case other positions become available
  • Business correspondence — As required by our record-keeping policies

After that, we securely delete or anonymize it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what we have — Request a copy of your personal information
  • Fix mistakes — Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data — Request that we delete your information (with some exceptions)
  • Unsubscribe — Stop receiving emails from us at any time
  • Ask questions — Contact us about how we handle your information

To exercise any of these rights, just email us at [email protected] or call our office.

Links to Other Websites

Our website links to other sites (like news articles or partner organizations). We're not responsible for their privacy practices. When you click a link to another site, their privacy policy applies instead of ours.

International Visitors

Biolyse operates from Canada. If you're visiting from another country, your information will be processed here under Canadian privacy laws, which may differ from your local regulations.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. If we make significant changes, we'll update the date at the top. For major changes, we might also send an email to let you know.

Questions About Your Privacy?

If you have concerns about how we handle your information, we're here to help.
