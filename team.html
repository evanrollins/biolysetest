Our Departments

Biolyse's success is built on the collaboration and expertise of specialized teams working together toward a common goal.

Research & Development

Our R&D team pioneers innovative approaches to drug development, focusing on both new therapeutics and optimized generic formulations.

Key Functions: Formulation development, analytical method development, process optimization, stability testing

Manufacturing

Our manufacturing team operates state-of-the-art facilities producing sterile injectable medicines under the strictest quality standards.

Key Functions: Sterile production, aseptic processing, equipment maintenance, production planning

Quality Assurance

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. Our QA team ensures every product meets rigorous international standards.

Key Functions: Quality control testing, batch release, GMP compliance, deviation management

Regulatory Affairs

Our regulatory experts navigate complex approval processes to bring treatments to patients across multiple markets.

Key Functions: Regulatory submissions, compliance monitoring, product registration, agency communications

Business Development

Building partnerships and expanding access to our products through strategic relationships with distributors and healthcare organizations.

Key Functions: Partnership development, market analysis, contract negotiation, client relations

Human Resources

Supporting our greatest asset—our people—through recruitment, development, and creating a collaborative work environment.

Key Functions: Talent acquisition, employee development, benefits administration, workplace culture
