Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 4, 2024

At Biolyse Pharma Corp., your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information is collected when you visit our website, how it may be used, and your rights regarding that information.

This website is informational in nature. It does not offer products or services for sale online, and the primary means of contacting us is by email or phone.

About This Website and Our Hosting Provider

This website is hosted by IONOS (1&1 IONOS Inc.), a third-party web hosting provider. As a standard part of hosting and delivering websites, IONOS may automatically collect certain technical data from visitors, including:

  • IP addresses and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Date, time, and duration of visits
  • Pages and files accessed
  • Referring URLs (how you arrived at our site)

This data is collected and processed by IONOS as part of their infrastructure operations. Biolyse Pharma Corp. does not directly control this collection and does not have routine access to individual-level visitor logs. For details on how IONOS handles this data, please refer to the IONOS Privacy Policy.

Information You Provide to Us

We only receive personal information when you choose to contact us directly. This may include:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your company name and professional title
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • The content of your message or inquiry
  • Resume and work history (if inquiring about employment)

Contact on this website is initiated by emailing us directly. We do not operate online contact forms that submit data to a server at this time.

How We Use Your Information

Any information you send us directly is used solely to:

  • Respond to your questions and inquiries
  • Provide information you have requested about our products or services
  • Review employment inquiries and follow up as appropriate
  • Fulfil any other purpose you contacted us for

We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your consent, and we will not contact you for reasons unrelated to your original inquiry.

No Online Transactions

This website does not include any purchasing functionality. No payment information, credit card data, or financial details are collected or processed through this site.

Who We Share Information With

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share it only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — Third parties who assist with business operations (such as our hosting provider, IONOS) are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing terms.
  • Legal obligations — If required by applicable law or a valid legal process.
  • Business changes — In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, personal information may transfer to the successor organization.

Cookies

Our hosting provider, IONOS, may place technical cookies on your device as part of standard website delivery. These are typically used to maintain session integrity and basic site functionality. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies directly.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect how some pages display.

How We Protect Your Information

Emails you send us are handled with care and stored securely within our internal systems. Access to correspondence is limited to staff who need it to respond to your inquiry.

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic communication is entirely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of information transmitted over the internet.

How Long We Keep Your Information

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected:

  • General inquiries and correspondence — typically 2–3 years, or until the matter is resolved
  • Employment inquiries — up to 2 years in the event a relevant position becomes available
  • Business records — as required under applicable regulations and internal record-keeping policies

After the applicable retention period, information is securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights

Under applicable Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Withdrawal of consent — Withdraw consent to our use of your information, subject to legal or contractual restrictions
  • Inquire — Ask questions about our privacy practices

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us by email or phone using the details below.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites, such as news articles or partner organizations. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. When you follow a link to another website, that site's privacy policy governs.

International Visitors

Biolyse Pharma Corp. is based in Canada. If you visit or contact us from outside Canada, please be aware that your information will be processed in Canada under Canadian privacy laws, which may differ from the laws of your home country.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we do, the effective date at the top of this page will be revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
