About Us

Our Mission

Advancing Cancer Care Through Innovation and Accessibility

Our mission is to ensure the continued distribution of our life-saving medicines while simultaneously pioneering innovative and effective treatments to enrich the lives of patients worldwide.

At Biolyse, our name is our inspiration. Biolyse means "the separation of biological substances into their component parts". Using the techniques of isolation and purification, we have been producing naturally based medical products and chemical extracts of high quality and concentration for over twenty years.

We believe that the objectives of the pharmaceutical industry should be simple: a dedication to science, and a desire to benefit humanity. While large pharmaceutical companies can sometimes lose sight of these objectives as scientific discovery takes a backseat to profitability, Biolyse maintains the ideals of simpler times.

Biolyse endeavors to provide quality products of the purest form, available to the consumer market at reasonable prices. We adhere to principles of ethics, hard work, and continual innovation at all levels of our company. We do not forget that our institution is based on the health of real people, whose well-being is more than important to us.

Our Future

Expanding Horizons in Biologic Medications

While we prioritize our licensed products, Biolyse Pharma is equally dedicated to advancing into the development of biologic medications. Committed to collaborating with the pharmaceutical industry, we strive to develop future products, processes, and services that benefit patients worldwide.

Our biologics facility, presently in its last stage of completion, will expand our capabilities to produce large molecules such as:

  • Monoclonal antibodies
  • Therapeutic peptides
  • Immunologic modulators

At Biolyse, we are committed to returning our profits back into continual research and development, in an effort to fight disease, and in the hope to help create a brighter tomorrow.

We recognize the importance of continued exploration to find valuable new natural compounds in place of more costly proprietary synthetic technologies. Biolyse extracts, concentrates and purifies natural bioactive compounds with a vision towards sustainable pharmaceutical development.

Our History

Two Decades of Innovation and Excellence

Biolyse Pharma Corporation has been at the forefront of pharmaceutical innovation, dedicated to making life-saving cancer treatments accessible and affordable to patients worldwide.

1999
Biolyse located its head office in St. Catharines, Ontario, in the Niagara peninsula. Our proximity to Toronto, Buffalo and Chicago allows us to better meet the demands of international markets.
Early 2000s
Our main focus became the development of Paclitaxel for Injection, a chemotherapeutic treatment used in the fight against various forms of cancer. By concentrating on this one cancer medication and thoroughly investigating every aspect of its development, Biolyse manufactured Paclitaxel efficiently while maintaining a lower cost to our ultimate consumers — the cancer patients who so badly need it.
2005-2006
Exciting developments at Biolyse Pharma placed us at the forefront of the Canadian pharmaceutical industry in the fight against a possible influenza pandemic. Biolyse engineers developed a generic low-cost version of the anti-viral medication oseltamivir and secured a plentiful and stable supply of the active component within Canada.
Present Day
Today, Biolyse manufactures and distributes sterile injectable medicines to oncology centers nationally and internationally. We remain open to collaborations and committed to developing high-quality pharmaceuticals at an accessible cost. We continue expanding our product line while maintaining our commitment to quality, affordability, and patient well-being.

Biolyse Pharma remains open to collaborations and committed to developing high-quality pharmaceuticals at an accessible cost. We believe that, by expanding our products and continually refining our unique methods of extraction and purification, we can provide solutions to a varied customer base.
